yo 'yaṃ saṃvṛtisaṃghaḥ labhyam anena dharmasaṃjñinā adharmeṇa karmaṇā kartuṃ

yas tv ayaṃ paramārthasaṃgha na labhyam anena adharmeṇa karmaṇā kartum* ||

poṣadhasthāpanavastu samāptaḥ ||
